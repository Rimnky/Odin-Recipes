French Fries

Description

French fries is a side dish or snack made from deep-fried potatoes that are evenly cut into strips. Most of the time it is served with other sauces like ketchup ans mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Evenly sized strips of potatoes
  • Vegitable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the potato strips in a large bowl of water for 30 minutes
  2. Pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry
  3. Heat oil in deep fryer to 135 degrees C
  4. Add the potatoes to the hot oil
  5. Fry about 5 minutes by stirring and flipping the potatoes
  6. Use a slotted spoon to transfer potatoes to a paper towel-lined plate
  7. Heat oil again to 175 degrees C
  8. Add the potatoes and fry them for a second time until golden brown
  9. Remove from the deep-fryer and blot with a paper towel
  10. Sprinkle with salt to serve
